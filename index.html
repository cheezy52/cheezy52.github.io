About Me

I am a junior web developer in the San Francisco Bay Area. I enjoy the experience of problem-solving and creation that comes with every new coding challenge, despite the frustration that often comes along with it as well.

I originally went to Harvey Mudd College and obtained my B.S. in engineering with a structural/mechanical focus, thereafter working for Space Systems/Loral for several years. However, after marrying my wife and our move into our new house, the commute from my new residence proved untenable in the long term. As a result, given my longtime hobby interest in programming and the relative dearth of nearby jobs in my prior career, it seemed an appropriate time to develop that hobby into a profession.
